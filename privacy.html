Privacy Policy

Last updated — July 2026

This policy explains what information TICKORA ("we," "us," "our") collects when you use tickora.com, why we collect it, and what rights you have over it. TICKORA is based in the United Arab Emirates and sells watches directly to customers, primarily via this website and WhatsApp.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information in three ways:

  • Account information. If you create an account, we collect your name, email address, and password. Your password is never stored in plain text — it's handled by our authentication provider (Supabase), which stores it securely hashed.
  • Usage data. We log basic activity on the site — page visits, which products you view, when you add an item to your cart, and when you click "Get a Quotation." If you're logged in, this activity is linked to your account; if not, it's linked to an anonymous session identifier stored in your browser.
  • WhatsApp communication. When you click a "Get a Quotation" button, you're taken to WhatsApp with a pre-filled message. Any conversation that follows happens directly on WhatsApp and is subject to WhatsApp's (Meta's) own privacy policy, not this one.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account
  • To follow up on quotation requests and answer questions about an order
  • To understand which watches and pages get the most interest, so we can improve the site and our catalog
  • To send you the occasional email — such as a first-order discount — if you've opted in via the newsletter form

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

3. Where Your Data Is Stored

Account details and site activity are stored with Supabase, our database and authentication provider. Data may be processed on servers outside the UAE as part of Supabase's infrastructure. We only use vendors who provide reasonable security safeguards for the data they hold on our behalf.

4. Cookies & Local Storage

We don't use tracking cookies. We do use your browser's local storage to remember your cart contents and an anonymous session ID between visits — this stays on your device and is not sold or shared.

5. Your Rights

You can ask us to access, correct, or delete the personal information we hold about you at any time. To do so, message us on WhatsApp or email us using the contact details below, and we'll act on your request within a reasonable time.

6. Children's Privacy

TICKORA is not directed at children, and we don't knowingly collect information from anyone under 18.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as the site or our practices change. If we make material changes, we'll update the "last updated" date above.

8. Contact Us

Questions about this policy or your data? Reach us on WhatsApp at +971 56 817 1463.
